Different kinds of Companies

  • Offices in Different Time Zones

-You work later so that you are in sync with the
other office ex: Belgrade --> San Francisco.

  • All in one office

-Everyone is together so you can ask for help
and you have proper contact : no need to video call ...

  • Work at home

-You are alone, you must make conference calls quite often, and you dont have
contact with the other offices (exept if you travel there).
